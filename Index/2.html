About me

I was born in the year 2000 as Adrian Branz and grew up in the southern german countryside. I was born with a disability on my right arm, that is why I had to develop my own techniques on every instrument I play.
When I was 8 years old I first started to learn classical guitar and I immediately fell in love with music. Some years later I was introduced to the electric guitar and the drums, a bit later to singing.
This was the time when I started playing and singing in band projects and composing original songs. At the age of 16 I started play guitar in a BigBand, so my interest and studies in jazz music started to influence my playing and composing style. With lots of years of practice and my a-levels I moved to Mannheim to grow my musical knowledge and reach. After some successful time in the cover music business I changed my artist name to Van Haezeler and started to compose on a debut EP. Since September 2020 I'm studying Singer/Songwriter and Guitar in Dinkelsbuehl (Germany).
